ARCHITECTURAL

Chemical Etching For Architecture

Trade Etching Direct are the UK's leading specialists in chemical etching for architecture.


By combining our expertise and years of experience in chemical etching and metal patination, we offer a near limitless range of unique custom architectural metal surfaces.


We can etch a vast range of metals, in sheets up to 2500mm x 1250mm. From vectored artwork, we can etch virtually any shape, pattern, logo, or image desired, producing genuinely unique architectural metal designs.


We expertly apply a wide range of patina finishes to create bespoke designs. Materials we work with include Brass, Bronze, Rimex Metals®, Steel (stainless, mirrored, & Corten), Aluminium, Copper, and more.


In addition to etching and patination, our in-house services include CNC routing, laser cutting, and digital printing.


The Grand Lobby - Cunard's Queen Anne

Trade Etching Direct etched and patinated the 97 brass panels that form Canadian artist Ian Kirkpatrick's stunning mural 'A Cunard Journey' onboard Cunard’s Queen Anne.


‘A Cunard Journey’ is an Art Deco celebration of the golden age of liner travel, situated in the heart of Queen Anne’s Grand Lobby.


Covering an area of 391 sq. m, the highly detailed etched panels were bent and assembled to create an impressive lenticular centerpiece.


This dynamic installation comes to life, showcasing three distinct artworks as guests traverse the space.


Lighting effects throughout the day bring out different qualities in the brass material, which appears light and welcoming in the morning, gradually becoming dimmer and more glamorous in the evening.
