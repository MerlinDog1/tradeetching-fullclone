Industrial Metal Nameplates, Tags & Control Panels: Precision etched and laser cut


Trade Etching Direct is your specialist UK trade partner, dedicated to providing superior quality etched metal solutions and precision laser-cut components for industrial nameplates, tags, and control panels. 


In today's demanding industrial environments, the need for durable, precise, and clearly legible identification and control components is paramount.


 From conveying vital operational information and ensuring safety compliance to maintaining brand integrity on your valuable equipment and machinery, the quality of your nameplates, tags, and control panels cannot be compromised.

Etched Stainless Steel & Brass Nameplates, Data Plates & Tags

Essential for branding, OEM identification, model/serial numbers, and critical operational data on all types of machinery and equipment. Our etched nameplates offer outstanding durability and legibility, even in challenging environments.


Our chemically etched labels are permanently etched directly into the panel surface. This method ensures exceptional durability and legibility. We offer a wide range of infill colours for high contrast and clear visibility.

Applications for Laser-Cut Products:



  • Control Panel Fascias & Backplates: Precisely cut openings for switches, dials, LEDs, and displays, ensuring perfect component fit.
  • Custom Shaped Tags & Nameplates: Create unique, intricate, or non-standard shapes.
  • Stencils and Templates: Durable and accurate stencils.
  • Bespoke Industrial Components: Custom-designed parts requiring high precision.
