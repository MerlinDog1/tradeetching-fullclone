LETTERS

Exquisite Flat Laser Cut Metal Letters: Solid Brass & Stainless Steel for Architectural Signage

At Trade Etching Direct, we manufacture premium flat cut metal letters that provide a definitive statement of quality for the most discerning projects.


We partner with architects, designers, and brand guardians to create architectural signage that conveys a sense of permanence, prestige, and meticulous attention to detail.


Our letters are precision laser cut from the finest solid metal plates ensuring unparalleled beauty and longevity for both interior and exterior environments.


 From the lobbies of luxury hotels and corporate headquarters to the fascias of high-end retail, our lettering transforms architectural vision into tangible excellence.

The Benchmark in Architectural Metal Lettering

Our Materials & Finishes: A Palette for Design Professionals

Solid Brass Lettering

The classic choice for warmth and sophistication, our solid brass letters are available in a range of finishes to suit your project's aesthetic.


  • Polished Brass: A flawless, mirror-like finish that delivers the ultimate statement of luxury and captures light with brilliant clarity.


  • Brushed (Satin) Brass: A sophisticated and versatile finish featuring a fine, unidirectional grain that provides a soft, elegant sheen.


  • Antique & Patinated Brass: Our specialty. We employ a multi-stage, in-house artisan process to create authentically aged, oxidized, and oil-rubbed finishes. This technique imparts a deep, characterful patina, perfect for heritage projects or for adding a unique, time-honored aesthetic to modern spaces

Stainless Steel Lettering

For a clean, modern aesthetic and exceptional durability, our stainless steel letters are the industry benchmark.


  • Polished Stainless Steel: A bright, contemporary mirror finish that creates a sharp, high-impact look.


  • Brushed (Satin) Stainless Steel: The professional standard for architectural signage, offering a clean, durable, and sophisticated appearance with a subtle lustre.


  • Material Grades for Performance: We specify our materials to ensure lasting performance. We use 304 Grade Stainless Steel as our high-quality standard for all applications.


  • For projects in coastal locations or other demanding exterior environments, we recommend and supply 316 Marine Grade Stainless Steel for its superior corrosion resistance


Pushing the Envelope

Advanced Customization & Bespoke Detailing

For projects that demand a truly unique identity, we go beyond standard lettering to offer a suite of advanced customization services. Our technical capabilities allow us to transform metal letters into intricate pieces of art, fully integrated with your design narrative and functional requirements.


Surface Etching: We can chemically etch complex patterns and decorative motifs directly onto the face of laser cut letters.

Imagine a hotel's signage featuring a subtle, etched local map, or a corporate logo with an intricate background pattern, adding a layer of storytelling and sophistication to the brand identity.
