Metal Patina Service

Expert metal patination for your project

Trade Etching Direct offer our customers first class professional metal patination service in-house. Whatever your project we can provide exceptional metal finishes to take it to the next level. We can combine our expertise in chemical etching and patination to produce stunning results. Typical projects we work with include:
 
◈ Signs and Plaques
◈ Memorials
◈ Cladding
◈ Splash backs
◈ Architectural Projects
◈ Railings
◈ Restoration Projects
◈ Interior Design Projects
◈ Film/TV props

We can work with a variety of metals including:

 ◈ Brass
 ◈ Bronze
 ◈ Copper
 ◈ Zinc
 ◈ Aluminium
 ◈ Stainless Steel
 ◈ Corten Steel
