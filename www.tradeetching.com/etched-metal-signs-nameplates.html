The UK's chemical etching experts for metal signs and nameplates

Chemical etching is a method of engraving that uses a high pressure, temperature regulated, chemical spray to remove material to create a permanent etched image or shape in metal. A UV sensitive resist is then applied to the surface of the material and is selectively removed using a photographic process, to expose the areas to be etched.

This process allows for reproduction of exceptionally detailed artwork. There is no heat distortion as associated with laser processing, and no tool wear, as with mechanical engraving methods. 

In the etching process the material is dissolved atom by atom, producing a smooth burr free finish and leaving the material grain structure unaffected.

TRADE ETCHING DIRECT FAQ

  • Who are Trade Etching Direct?

    Trade Etching Direct are a chemical etching company based in Corby, UK. We offer a first class trade-only chemical etching service to the UK sign industry. Founded in 2012 and operating out of a state of the art 4,000sq.ft facility we have the capacity to handle orders running to many thousands of pieces. 

  • What are the applications for chemical etching in the sign industry?

    Typical applications for chemical etching include:

    • Hotel & office signage
    • Wayfinding  signage
    • Industrial nameplates & labels
    • Identification products
    • Architectural panels
    • Plaques & memorials
    • Interpretation boards
    • Corporate awards
    • Safety & compliance signs
    • Promotional items
    • Thin metal components

  • What materials can T.E.D. chemically etch?

    Trade Etching Direct can chemically etch a wide variety of metals. Typical metals requested by our customers include:

    • Stainless steel
    • Brass
    • Bronze
    • Copper
    • Nickel
    • Zinc
    • Aluminium
    • Mild Steel
    • Rimex Metals® incl T22™ range

    Trade Etching Direct have the ability in-house to etch panels up to a maximum size of 4m x 2m.
